Chapter 3 Solow Growth model

Introduction

Robert Solow, 1987 Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences, starts from a general production function, giving at any point in time output \(Y_t\) as a function of inputs, capital \(K_t\) and labor \(L_t\): \[Y_t=F\left(K_t,L_t\right).\]
