Presentations. The presentations will take place during the last two sessions, on November 13 and November 27. Most of the papers are taken from a symposium on “The Top 1 Percent” in the Journal of Economic Perspectives. The papers will be presented by groups of 2. Each group should present for about 15 to 20 minutes. We will then have a 5 to 10 minutes classroom discussion on the paper. Your presentations should include at least a summary of the paper, and an overview of the main points. A critical take, as well as your personal thoughts on the paper, are strongly encouraged.
