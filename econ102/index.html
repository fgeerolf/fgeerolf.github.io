Past exams. Below is a list of past exams. Beware ! The ordering, and the content of the class are different this year compared to previous years, and increasingly so as you go back in time - in 2015 and 2016, I was using Chad Jones’ Macroeconomics textbook. Anything that I do not go over this quarter is not exam material. In order to succeed in the class, it is best to first and primarily review the material that is found in the lectures and the problem sets - that is probably where “marginal returns” are maximal.
